This delicious tiramisu made with homemade mascarpone custard (no raw eggs), layers of whipped cream, and rum and coffee-soaked ladyfingers is an impressive Italian dessert. Dust the top of the tiramisu with cocoa powder before serving.

Tiramisu, with its irresistible coffee flavor and lightly sweetened mascarpone, will never go out of style. This tiramisu recipe is a no-bake dessert that's sure to impress even the pickiest of eaters.

What Is Tiramisu?

Tiramisu is a coffee-flavored dessert that features layers of homemade whipped cream, an egg yolk-enriched mascarpone filling, and coffee-soaked ladyfingers.

Tiramisu Pronunciation

Not sure how to pronounce tiramisu? Order in confidence by saying "tee-ruh-mee-soo."

How to Make Tiramisu

Ready to make the best tiramisu of your life? You'll find the full recipe below, but here's what you can expect when you make this classic tiramisu recipe:

  1. Make the Filling: Cook the egg yolks, sugar, and milk until slightly thickened. Let cool slightly, then chill in the fridge for about an hour. When the filling has fully chilled, mix in mascarpone cheese.
  2. Make the Whipped Cream: Beat heavy cream with vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  3. Soak the Ladyfingers: Combine coffee and rum in a shallow dish. Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the mixture, making sure not to soak them too long, then arrange them in a single layer in a serving dish.
  4. Assemble the Layers: Spread half of the mascarpone filling over the ladyfingers. Add another layer of soaked ladyfingers, then top with the remaining mascarpone mixture.

How to Store Tiramisu

Cover the tiramisu tightly with plastic wrap or transfer it to an airtight container. Store in the refrigerator for up to 4 days. For best texture and flavor, allow the tiramisu to chill for several hours or overnight before serving. Tiramisu can also be frozen for up to 3 months; thaw overnight in the refrigerator before serving.

Can You Freeze Tiramisu?

Yes, you can absolutely freeze tiramisu. Wrap the tiramisu in a layer of plastic wrap and then a layer of aluminum foil. Freeze for up to three months. Thaw in the fridge overnight.

Ingredients

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ⅔ cup milk
  • 1 ¼ cups heavy cream
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pound mascarpone cheese, at room temperature
  • ¼ cup strong brewed coffee, at room temperature
  • 2 tablespoons rum
  • 2 (3 ounce) packages ladyfinger cookies
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
